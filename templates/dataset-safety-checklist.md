## Checks

- [ ] Status is explicit and truthful.
- [ ] No dataset files, raw data, private datasets, unapproved sanitized samples, private telemetry, or source exports are included.
- [ ] Donor data, student data, school private data, volunteer data, customer data, and private Foundation operations are excluded.
- [ ] Source, license, consent, or publication basis is documented.
- [ ] PII and re-identification risk review is complete.
- [ ] Allowed and forbidden uses are stated.
- [ ] Maintenance, correction, withdrawal, and deprecation handling are documented.
- [ ] Release claims have public reviewed evidence.
- [ ] Human data governance authority approved any `Released` status.
